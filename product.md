# 🪙 moniq — Personal Finance Tracker

## Product Philosophy (put this in `PRODUCT.md`)

**moniq** is a **manual-first, highly customizable personal finance app** that helps users track money across custom sources, payment methods, categories, and goals — without forcing bank integrations.

Principles:

* Manual > Automated (user stays in control)
* Customizable > Opinionated
* Simple core, expandable architecture
* Global-first (no country assumptions)

Target users:

* People frustrated with rigid finance apps
