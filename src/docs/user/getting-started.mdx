# Getting Started with Moniq

Welcome to Moniq, your personal finance companion. Moniq is designed to be simple, privacy-focused, and powerful.

## Core Concepts

- **Accounts**: Your bank accounts, wallets, or cash.
- **Transactions**: Every inflow or outflow of money.
- **Categories**: Organize your spending.
- **Budgets**: Plan your spending.

## First Steps

1. **Add an Account**: Go to Settings > Accounts to add your first account.
2. **Import Data**: You can sync with Google Sheets to keep your data safe.
3. **Log a Transaction**: Use the "Add Transaction" button to record your first expense.

## Privacy

Moniq stores your data locally in your browser and optionally syncs with your own Google Drive. We never see your data.
