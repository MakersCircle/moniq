# Common Scenarios

Here are some common financial scenarios and how to handle them in Moniq.

## 💳 Credit Card Payments

When you pay your credit card bill, it's a **Transfer** from your Savings account to your Credit Card account.
1. Click "Add Transaction".
2. Select "Transfer" type.
3. From: "Savings Account".
4. To: "Credit Card".

## 💸 Lending Money

When you lend money to a friend, it's an **Expense** from your account to a "Lend" category.
1. Create a category called "Lend" under the "Lend" group.
2. Record an Expense to this category.
3. When they pay you back, record **Income** from the same "Lend" category.

## 🏦 Fixed Deposits

Treat a Fixed Deposit as a separate **Account**.
- Transfer money from your Savings to the FD account.
- When you receive interest, record it as **Income** directly into the FD account.

## 🛍️ Split Expenses

If you buy something and someone owes you half:
1. Record the full amount as an Expense.
2. When they pay you back, record it as Income (or a negative expense if you prefer to balance the category).
